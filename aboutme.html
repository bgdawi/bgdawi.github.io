A Little Bit About Me

My name is Brian and I am originally from Asheville NC. When I turned 18, I moved out of the house and enrolled in college at UNC Charlotte. I graduated in 2013 with a B.A in Criminal Justice. After working a few dead-end jobs after graduation I decided to enlist in the Military. I am currently serving the last 6 months of my contract here in Chesapeake, VA. I’m married to a beautiful wife, and I have a newborn daughter!

Roughly 2 years ago I started my journey into the IT field by enrolling at ECPI University in Virginia Beach, VA. Since then I have received 2 IT certifications and was selected for a Microsoft training program Microsoft Software and System Academy to train individuals to be proficient in cloud administration, and engineering roles.

I will be transitioning out of the military in February of 2023. I am currently working on a few projects and I’m always looking for new ways to increase my knowledge and learn new things!
